Paradox's Gambit

Context:

My story is about a person named Kiero, a historian, discovers that his organization, the time-library, is secretly destroying and ripping history apart for sports and jokes rather than preserving and protecting it. A powerful machine knows that something is wrong and warns him of this betrayal, forcing him to abandon his tedious job and become a fugitive. Kiero wages a concealed war/fight against his former employers and bosses. He fights alone to stop them from destroying the integrity and the universe.

EXTRA : Kiero becomes an outlaw/outcast fighting his former bosses to restore time's integrity.

Content:

I created my movie webpage idea or portfolio using what I learned from HTML (mostly) and CSS. I am looking to have mostly of include headings, paragraphs. This assignment primarily or mostly focuses on demonstrating my ability to structure my content using appropriate HTML tags for different sections, such as a main header (h1), (h2), or/and body text (p). I used CSS to manage the (basic) visual presentation, including colors, and the overall layout. For each section I tried to include a clear main part and a brief description, so anyone reading my page would kind of get the idea of what my assignment idea is about.
